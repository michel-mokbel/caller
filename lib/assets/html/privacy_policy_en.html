Privacy Policy for Vault Book

Effective Date: March 14, 2024

At Vault Book, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our contact management application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: We access your device's contacts with your permission to provide contact management features.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information to improve our service and fix issues.
  • Device Information: Technical details about your device to ensure compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide contact management and organization features
  • Enable blocking and filtering of unwanted contacts
  • Maintain and improve app functionality
  • Protect your sensitive contact information
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

3. Data Security

  • Contact Encryption: Secure contacts are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods.
  • Local Storage: Your contact data is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • No External Sharing: We do not sell or share your contact information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following services to enhance functionality:

  • AppsFlyer: For app analytics and attribution tracking. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase: For app performance monitoring and crash reporting. Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Control app permissions through your device settings
  • Choose which contacts to encrypt or secure
  • Manage blocked numbers and contacts
  • Delete your secure contacts at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Vault Book is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Permission Usage

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Contacts: To read and manage your contact list
  • Storage: To store encrypted contacts and settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
